What we do

We take a practical approach to engineering, helping teams ship faster, operate more safely, and reduce costs without compromising on quality.

Cloud-Native Platforms & Modernization

Public Cloud Consulting

Architecture design, modernization, migration, and cost optimization across AWS, Azure, and GCP environments, ensuring performance, reliability, and governance.

Private Cloud Solutions

Deployment and operations of modern on-prem infrastructure with OpenStack, Proxmox, and Ceph; built for performance, resilience, and autonomy. Expertise includes compute, networking, and object storage design and integration.

Platform Engineering

Self-serve developer platforms on Kubernetes or OpenShift with GitOps (Flux/Argo), secure supply chain, and policy automation.

GPU / AI Infrastructure

Engineered platforms built on OpenStack | KubeVirt for K8saaS, and KubeFlow for accelerated data/ML, GPU scheduling, observability, and tenancy.

Migrations from VMware

Warm migrations into Private Cloud Solutions built using Open Source technologies such as OpenStack, KubeVirt or Proxmox: snapshots, continuous replication, and planned cutover with minimal downtime.

Enterprise Infrastructure & Services

Security Engineering

Perimeter and network defense using open-source firewalls and IDS/IPS like IPFire, OpnSense, pfSense, and Snort. Implementation of Zero Trust access and VPN frameworks such as Pritunl, OpenVPN, and IPsec for site-to-site and remote workforce connectivity.

End-User Computing

Design and deployment of secure, scalable virtual desktop infrastructure (VDI) environments using open-source technologies, enabling centralized management, remote access, and data protection.

Core Infrastructure & Collaboration Services

Enterprise-ready core infrastructure services including Active Directory, LDAP, DNS, DHCP, and email systems, built to integrate seamlessly with modern cloud platforms and deliver secure identity, policy control, and collaboration for distributed teams.

Staff Augmentation

Extend your in-house teams with experienced engineers specialized in Kubernetes, SRE, OpenStack, Proxmox, networking, security, and DevOps automation. We align talent to your project’s lifecycle, accelerating delivery without long-term overhead.
